It’s a surf camp baby!

Experience the iconic surf culture of the West Coast as part of our Teen California Surf and Adventure Camp. Travel to the state that helped revolutionize the sport and learn to surf in the wake of legends. Our unique program will take you away from the crowds to the famous Central Coast where you will be soaked in both the waves and the amazingly diverse marine environment. 

We will travel the coastline from the Monterey Bay Aquarium down to the Santa Barbara Channel, staying in cabins and camping in tents to experience all the beauty California has to offer.
